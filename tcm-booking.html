Medical & Wellness Booking

TCM Johnny

Role: Lead UI/UX Designer & Developer Stack: WordPress, Amelia Pro, Custom Scripting Visit Live Site

A highly specialized digital booking platform designed for a Traditional Chinese Medicine clinic to streamline patient onboarding and practitioner scheduling.

The Architecture

The core system relies on a single page architecture featuring an interactive service layout integrated directly with an enterprise grade scheduling engine. The front end UI acts as an intuitive gateway, mapping complex operational data into clean, accessible touchpoints for patients seeking targeted holistic treatments.

The Trenches

The primary challenge was managing exceptionally rigid clinical rules without any initial design direction or brand guidelines from the client. The system required complex features that extended far beyond what standard booking plugins can provide natively. We had to accommodate strict parameters, including an explicit fifteen days advanced waiting time window, direct patient matching to specific practitioners, customized booking rejection rules, automated email hooks, and a tailored administrative dashboard, all while ensuring the high security required for clinical operations.

The Study

The Core Objective

The main goal was to engineer a frictionless user experience that could gracefully enforce the clinic's strict scheduling rules without alienating the patient. Because medical booking can quickly feel transactional or intimidating, the interface needed to balance administrative rigidity with absolute user clarity, turning a complex multi step scheduling matrix into an effortless digital interaction.

The UX & Visual Identity

To solve the lack of visual direction, I designed an interface inspired directly by the physical menus found in high end Traditional Chinese Medicine clinics. The visual theme utilizes a tasteful, elevated Chinese design aesthetic, replacing clinical sterility with organic warmth and minimal styling. To guide the user through the strict booking parameters, the platform organizes content into a single page menu paired with a step by step scheduling onboarding flow. This approach systematically segments information, giving the user full transparency and confidence as they select their preferred practitioner and select their clinical window.

The Study: The Technical Execution

To deliver on the client's extensive feature requests, I deployed Amelia Pro as the core engine and enhanced it with advanced custom scripting. This hybrid implementation allowed us to override native limitations and successfully execute the complex scheduling logic. Custom code blocks were written to enforce the mandatory fifteen days advanced booking restriction, unlock specific employee selection capabilities, and integrate backend email automations that trigger instantly upon status changes. Furthermore, I built specific rejection parameters into the data validation layer, ensuring that invalid appointment requests are handled automatically, keeping the comprehensive administrative dashboard completely organized and accurate for the clinic staff.
